Platform overview

Three research products for investors who do the work.

01

Curated stock picks

A weekly research publication delivering three to five fully documented equity recommendations per issue, each including a thesis summary, financial model assumptions, upside and downside scenarios, key risk factors, and a suggested entry range. Picks are tracked from publication with ongoing updates when material news changes the thesis, and portfolio performance is published transparently including all losers, not just winners.

02

Equity screener

A multi-factor screener allowing subscribers to filter the North American equity universe by over 150 fundamental, technical, and sentiment criteria. Pre-built screens replicate popular investment strategies including deep value, GARP, dividend growth, insider buying, and short squeeze setups. Screener results export to a watchlist with automated alerts when screened securities cross user-defined thresholds on any tracked metric.

03

Research archive

A searchable library of all published research with full text, financial model attachments, and performance attribution showing what happened to each pick after publication. Historical picks are annotated with the material events that drove outcomes, allowing subscribers to evaluate the research process and identify which analyst frameworks have the strongest predictive track records before committing capital to new recommendations.
